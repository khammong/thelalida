About Project

Quartet specializes in managing technology for mid-sized companies. Most clients start with a technology audit and IT support package.  Many use our hosted infrastructure to augment on-site capabilities and launch development projects for customizations.  Then with a stable, scalable IT platform, they adopt our IT leadership services and really drive strategic advantage through IT.  Our services are scalable, enterprise grade and comprehensive. They are designed to support growth seamlessly and affordably.  Our focus has not changed since our inception in 1998  – to drive growth, drive efficiency and drive strategic advantage through technology.

Most services are delivered on a flat-fee, retainer basis. Retainers vary by number of users, types of services and required infrastructure.  We have a procurement service for hardware and software. Our detailed, yet understandable service reports are getting accolades from our clients.  We’ve invested heavily in remote support, work flow management and IT diagnostic tools.   Quartet is a comprehensive technology management firm designed to service mid-tier companies – we are accountable, responsive and reputable.  Click here to schedule a visit from a consultant to discuss your needs and start planning how you reach your goals.
