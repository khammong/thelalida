About Project

Pondichery.in is an online store of carefully curated antiques, vintage furniture and collectibles. Objects we list are curated for their design, craftsmanship and the stories they represent. Our primary focus is on 20th century vintage furniture – especially furniture that combines clean lines and form with functionality. Campaign Furniture, Art Deco and Mid Century Modern designs are some of our favourites.

Apart from antique furniture, we list a few objects that represent outstanding design, craftsmanship or innovation over the last century. These could be antique survey instruments used to build the British Empire, rare first edition books of pioneering explorers, lithographs, handcrafted wooden statues, etc. We source these objects from  private collectors and antique dealers across India.

Phantom Hands is a metaphor for all the unknown artisans over generations, who have contributed to the perfection of each object and artifact.
